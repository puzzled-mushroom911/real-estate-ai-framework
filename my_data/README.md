# Your Data

Drop your files here and Claude will build a personalized knowledge base from them. This is how Claude learns your voice, your market expertise, and your client history.

## What Goes Where

| Folder | What to put here | Examples |
|--------|-----------------|----------|
| `youtube/` | Nothing — Claude downloads these automatically when you provide your channel handle | Auto-populated |
| `podcasts/` | Audio files from your podcast episodes | .mp3, .m4a, .wav files |
| `reviews/` | Google Business or Zillow review exports | CSV exports, screenshots, or text files |
| `website/` | Nothing — Claude scrapes these automatically when you provide your URL | Auto-populated |
| `sales_history/` | Your recent sales data | CSV with columns: address, price, date, property_type |
| `leads/` | Your lead/contact database export | CSV with columns: name, email, phone, source, status |
| `voice_samples/` | Audio recordings of you talking naturally | Voice memos, client calls (with permission), video audio |
| `documents/` | Any other documents that represent your expertise | Market reports, guides, PDFs, presentation decks |

## How It Works

1. Drop files in the right folders
2. Tell Claude: "Build my knowledge base from my data"
3. Claude processes everything and makes it searchable
4. Now when Claude writes content or responds to clients, it sounds like YOU

## Privacy

All processing happens on your computer. Your data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly connect a cloud service.
